Hyperdraft Turn your notes into a website

An app for organizing your thoughts.

Inspired by Notational Velocity, it is highly keyboard-focused and allows you to link between notes. Additionally, it can generate a website from your public notes.

Open app

Features

Network your thoughts Link between notes via [[wiki]] syntax
Make a website Publish notes to the internet via remoteStorage
Jump around Navigate large documents via headings
Focus on what's current Archive infrequently used notes
Your data on all your devices Cloud sync with remoteStorage
No Wi-Fi, no problem Works offline without internet access
Not creepy No ads, behavioural analytics, 3rd party trackers
Accessible anywhere Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices
